Overview Playground Guide

Understand SARIMA choices through interactive ACF & PACF patterns

SARIMA models seasonality and short-term correlation in time-series data. ACF (Autocorrelation Function) helps reveal moving-average behavior, while PACF (Partial Autocorrelation Function) helps reveal autoregressive behavior.

This app is designed to help you connect visual spike patterns with SARIMA parameter selection: non-seasonal terms p and q, plus seasonal terms P and Q.
